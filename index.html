Your Defense Framework

This framework provides a holistic view of managing cybersecurity risk. The five pillars represent the full lifecycle of a potential incident, from preparation to recovery.

Building a Cyber-Aware Culture

Technology is only one part of our defense. Our people are our "human firewall," and fostering a culture of awareness is our greatest strength.

Ongoing Training

Cybersecurity isn't a one-time event. Provide regular training, updates, and reminders to keep awareness high for all staff members.

Storytelling

Share real-world examples and stories of cyber threats. This powerful, culturally relevant teaching method makes risks tangible and lessons memorable.

See Something, Say Something

Create a safe environment where employees feel comfortable reporting suspicious activity immediately, without fear of blame or repercussion.

Lead by Example

Leadership must actively champion cybersecurity. When leaders follow security protocols diligently, it sets the standard for the entire organization.
